Why Writing Skills Are Important in Marketing?
Much of marketing communication is text. Think blogs, straplines, knowledge bases, press releases and whitepapers, for example.
Basically, you can not shy away from writing if you are a marketer.


Which Writing Skills Should You Have Writing is a broad practice with plenty of different specialisms. As a marketer, you’ll need to be well-versed in all types of writing, utilised in different areas of a campaign and as part of the creation process. Generally speaking, there are four types of writing you will want to master: Persuasive Writing — Perhaps the most obvious of the four, you will need to know how to make propositions attractive to generate sales from your advertisements. Informational Writing — Marketers can often fall into sales-y scripture when sometimes clarity and accuracy is what is needed. Informational writing has never been so in demand. Formal Writing — Formal writing has always been heavily involved in marketing for professional pieces like journalistic reporting, press releases and whitepapers. However, continued scrutiny on data regulations and increased transparency are forcing a formal approach to become more commonplace as marketers explain policies, customer rights and legislation. Informal Writing — Most advertising is written in an informal style, as though brands are chatting to a group of friends. You will deploy informal writing in captivating email marketing headlines, entertaining blogs and engaging website copy to name a few formats.


Here are a few writing formats marketers will need to master:

• Blogs

•Articles

• Whitepapers

• Press Releases

• Email Marketing

• Social Media

• Advertising and flyers

That is right. Marketing managers, in particular, will need to sharpen their written skills to deliver effective communication to more than just their brand target audience. As a manager, you will need to be able to write compelling yet clear briefs for your team to follow and give eloquent and tactful feedback, as well as possess an entire range of other communication skills.
